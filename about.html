About This Calculator

Net pay from gross salary — five deductions, pay-frequency output, Ontario Health Premium included.

Why Net Pay Is Harder to Predict Than People Expect

Most Ontario employees have a reasonable sense of their gross salary. Their actual take-home is often several thousand dollars lower — and the gap isn't explained by a single number. Federal income tax, Ontario provincial tax, CPP contributions, EI premiums, and the Ontario Health Premium all come off independently, each with different rules and thresholds.

This calculator runs all five deductions in a single pass and returns your estimated net pay — annually, monthly, bi-weekly, or per pay period — so the full picture is visible before a job offer is signed or a budget is built.

Who Uses This Calculator

Comparing two job offers stated in gross salary, checking whether a gross number meets a monthly budget, or understanding how much of a raise actually lands as take-home pay. The pay-frequency output is useful for employees who want to know what a new salary means per cheque, without doing the deduction math manually.

Limitations

Results are estimates based on 2026 basic personal amounts and standard full-year employment. These are not modelled and will affect actual take-home pay:

  • TD1 credits beyond the basic personal amount (spousal, disability, eligible dependant, etc.)
  • RRSP deductions or pension adjustments
  • Union dues, professional fees, or employer-side benefit costs
  • Mid-year employment starts — CPP and EI deductions depend on full-year earnings
  • Quebec — this tool applies Ontario provincial rates only
  • Self-employment income, investment income, or other non-employment sources

For employer payroll calculations, use the CRA's Payroll Deductions Online Calculator at canada.ca. For tax preparation, consult a qualified tax professional.

Privacy

No income figure you enter is sent anywhere. All calculations run locally in your browser. No account is needed, and no tracking cookies are set by this tool. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
