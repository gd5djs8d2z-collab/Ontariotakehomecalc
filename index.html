What This Calculator Does

This calculator estimates your take-home pay as an Ontario employee. It applies 2026 federal income tax, Ontario provincial income tax, CPP contributions (CPP1 and CPP2), EI premiums, and the Ontario Health Premium to your gross salary. Federal and Ontario basic personal amounts are applied automatically. Results show your estimated net pay per pay period, monthly, and annually. No other credits or deductions are assumed.

Calculation Methodology

This calculator uses 2026 CRA and Ontario rates. Federal and provincial taxes are calculated using progressive bracket systems with basic personal amounts as non-refundable credits. CPP1 and CPP2 contributions are applied to annual maximums. EI premiums are calculated on insurable earnings up to the annual ceiling. The Ontario Health Premium is applied by income tier. RRSP deductions, additional credits, and employer-specific arrangements are not included.
