Overview

What This Calculator Does

This calculator estimates your annual take-home pay as an Ontario employee by applying 2026 federal and provincial tax rules, CPP contributions, EI premiums, and the Ontario Health Premium to your gross income. All rates come from CRA and ESDC publications for the 2026 tax year.

Results show your estimated net pay per pay period, monthly, and annually. The basic personal amounts (federal and Ontario) are applied automatically. No other credits or deductions are assumed.

Methodology

Calculation Methodology

This calculator applies 2026 CRA and ESDC rates using progressive federal and Ontario bracket systems, with basic personal amounts as non-refundable credits. CPP1/CPP2 and EI are calculated to annual maximums. Ontario Health Premium is applied by income tier. RRSP, additional credits, and employer-specific deductions are not included.
